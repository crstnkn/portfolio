Art Direction, Content Strategy

In 2023, lululemon tapped BASIC® to solve a deceptively simple problem: guests on lululemon.com couldn't tell their leggings apart. With three distinct franchises each built for different uses, we used product photography and feature reels to do the differentiating. Contextual styling and props guide guests to the right fit, while close-up detail shots validate the choice, all while letting lululemon's personality shine through.
