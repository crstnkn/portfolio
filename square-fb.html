Campaign

Art Direction, Creative Concepting & Strategy

In 2025, the GTM team launched Square's next Food & Beverage campaign around a single insight: the magic comes from the sellers, and Square's job is to give them the time to make it. That became Here for the Good Stuff. We led creative strategy, a studio shoot where Square hardware took on the personality of the restaurants it lives in, and a lifestyle photo and video shoot. The hero video became the most viewed in Square's YouTube history, garnering 11 million views in its first month.
